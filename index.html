About Us

Creative Minds Library is a hub for lifelong learners. Our extensive collections, programs, and exhibitions open a gateway to over six centuries of global history—from the 15th century to today.

We welcome readers, researchers, and dreamers alike to connect with the past and shape the future. Whether you're here to explore literature, learn a new skill, or just enjoy a good book—we have something for you!
